About The Event

Discover the essence of Mark Us – 26

Description

Mark Us – 26 is a one-day intra-department symposium designed to bring together students, faculty, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements in computer technology.

Purpose

Our goal is to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We aim to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and creative ideas.

What to Expect

Engaging technical and non-technical events, insightful paper presentations, and opportunities to network with peers and mentors. Expect a day filled with learning and fun.

Who Can Participate

The symposium is open to all students of the Department of Computer Technology – PG. We encourage everyone, regardless of skill level, to participate.
