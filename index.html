About The Event

Description

Mesica ‚Äì 26 is a one-day intra-department symposium designed to bring together students, faculty, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements in computer technology.

Purpose

Our goal is to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We aim to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and creative ideas.

Who Can Participate

The symposium is open to all students of the Department of Computer Technology ‚Äì PG. We encourage everyone, regardless of skill level, to participate.

What to Expect

Engaging technical and non-technical events, insightful paper presentations, and opportunities to network with peers and mentors. Expect a day filled with learning and fun.
